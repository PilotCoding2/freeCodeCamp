Man Strikes a Cow - Again

Drunk man kills a cow for a second time.

By Cody Hernández

Neighbors reported a loud sound after an anonymous drunk man killed a cow after crashing with it, again. Authorities have reported that not a single person resulted hurt due to this accident, however, the owner of the cow asks for repairs.

The person responsible for the death of the cow is described as a male, 5'4", brown hair, wearing a lumberjack shirt. Even though this is one of the saddests moments in the history of Los Mochis, authorities seem to be working inefficiently towards solving the case.
